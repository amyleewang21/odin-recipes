Lasagna

Description

Yummy, delicious, scrumption tomatoes and cheese with pasta slabs oh wait no, that doesn't sound delicios. Or does it?

Put the tomatoes on the eggs and the fries with the mustard and mayo. Wait no, that's not right

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs
  • Dough
  • Meat or Squash
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix dough with eggs and make pasta sheets
  2. Mush up tomatoes to make tomato sauce
  3. Put meat
  4. Put Cheese
  5. Bake in oven
