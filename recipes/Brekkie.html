Breakfast Sandwich

Description

Yummy, delicious, scrumption cheese and tomatoes on toast

Good for the morning, evening, anytime

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Sriracha
  • Cream Cheese

Steps

  1. Put cream cheese on bread
  2. Put egg on cream cheese
  3. Put tomato slice on other bread
  4. Put sriracha on tomato slice
  5. Slap together
